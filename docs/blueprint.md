# **App Name**: Mila - Plantilla Viva

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Suggestion: Suggest improvements to normative documents using a generative AI tool that references applicable standards and legal requirements.
- Section Overview: Display a navigable list of document sections, each with a 'Completeness Score' and alert indicators for errors or inconsistencies.
- Intelligent Editing: Provide a side-by-side view of the original text and suggested revisions, with an option to apply, edit, or discard changes.
- Validation & Export: Show validation results (compliance score, alerts) and export options (PDF or report inclusion) in a dedicated panel.
- Comprehensive Reporting: Generate a comprehensive report that shows the suggestions, compliance percentages, and a final completeness index.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: #A3E635 (stable and uniform) for active validation background and main containers.
- Secondary color: #064E3B for sidebar and institutional headers.
- Neutral background: White (#FFFFFF), or #F8FAFC for secondary containers.
- Validation/Norms blue: #0EA5E9 or #0284C7 for active norms/documents labels and article highlighting.
- Action/Alert buttons: #F87171 (minor error), #DC2626 (major error) for 'previous' button and normative alerts.
- Warning yellow: #FACC15 for minor warnings.
- Technical texts dark blue: #1E3A8A for normative quotes, legal articles, and justifications.
- Sidebar: #064E3B with white icons and hover in #10B981.
- 'Validate Sheet' button: #0EA5E9 with white text.
- 'Download PDF' button: #DC2626 with white icon.
- 'Partial Results' box: #F8FAFC background with #CBD5E1 border.
- Severity labels: Medium: #FACC15. High: #F87171. Low: #A5F3FC.
- Use the Nunito font family for all text elements to ensure a clean and readable experience.
